About

Kultiv8 Consulting is your preparation and career coaching partner, founded in 2018 with a focus on personal growth through innovation.

With roughly over 50% of recent graduates unemployed or underemployed, our mission is to close that gap by offering guidance and coaching services that will increase the likelihood of securing employment. With our 7 years of HR and recruitment and having conducted 600+ candidate and client interviews; we aim to dispense all that knowledge to newly qualified and experienced professionals.

We have provided interview preparation, career coaching, CV writing and work-readiness sessions to newly qualified graduates all the way to PHD professionals with 10+ years experience. We don't teach, rather we help cultivate all that dormant potential that lies within you.
